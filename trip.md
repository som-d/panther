## 2. VISA & DOCUMENTS CHECKLIST

### Schengen Visa for Parents

Apply **8-10 weeks before**. Visa must cover full 84 days.

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Schengen visa fee (€90 × 2) | ~₹16,400 |
| VFS/TLS service fee | ~₹5,000 |
| Travel insurance (90 day, €30K min) | ~₹5,000 |
| Photos + misc | ~₹600 |
| **Total** | **~₹27,000** |

### Visa Tips

- **Accommodation proof**: Use **refundable hotel bookings** for visa application (Booking.com free cancellation). Cancel after visa is approved. Actual stay at Covilhã or cheaper hostels. This is common practice.
- **Flight bookings**: Show **hold/reservation** (not fully paid) if possible. Airline websites or travel agents can do this.
- **Timing**: Visa takes 15-30 days in peak season. Apply **at least 8-10 weeks before** the planned departure.
- **Local contact's address not used**: They may not have a rental contract, so don't show Covilhã as accommodation on visa application. Use hotels/hostels for the application.

### Documents Needed

- Passports (valid 3+ months beyond return, 2+ blank pages)
- Visa application form
- Travel insurance certificate
- Round-trip flight booking (India↔Lisbon)
- Refundable hotel bookings (for visa, cancel later)
- Bank statements (last 6 months)
- Travel itinerary (this document!)

### Entry

All countries are Schengen (Portugal, Spain, Italy). One visa covers everything.
