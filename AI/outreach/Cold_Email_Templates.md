## Cold Outreach Strategy Guide

### Step 1: Find the Right Person
- **Hiring Manager**: Search "Engineering Manager" OR "DevOps Manager" at target company on LinkedIn
- **Recruiter**: Search "Talent Acquisition" OR "Technical Recruiter" at target company
- **Peer referral**: Search "DevOps Engineer" at target company (ask for referral)

### Step 2: Personalize Your Email
Before sending, add 1 personalized line:
- "I noticed your team uses [Tool] - I've worked with similar tools like..."
- "I read about [Company initiative] - my experience in [area] could help..."
- "I see your team is growing - I'm looking for a role where I can..."

### Step 3: Send in Batches
- Week 1: 10-15 emails per day
- Week 2: Follow up on non-replies
- Track response rate - aim for 20-30%

### Step 4: Track Responses
| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| No reply | Email not seen or low priority | Follow up in 5-7 days |
| "Not hiring" | No current opening | Ask if they know anyone hiring |
| "Send resume" | Interested | Send immediately + thank |
| "Let's chat" | Hot lead | Book within 24 hours |
