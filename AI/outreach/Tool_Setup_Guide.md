## GMASS SETUP (10 minutes)

### Step 1: Install GMass
1. Open Chrome → go to **gmass.com**
2. Click "Install GMass for Chrome"
3. Grant Gmail access
4. You'll see a new "GMass" button in your Gmail compose window

### Step 2: Send Your First Tracked Email
1. Click "Compose" in Gmail
2. Write your email to `ankur.tewari@logicmonitor.com`
3. Instead of clicking "Send" → click the **GMass button** (looks like a rocket icon)
4. GMass will:
   - ✅ Track when they OPEN your email
   - ✅ Track if they CLICK any links
   - ✅ Auto-follow-up if no reply in 3 days (optional)

### Step 3: Create a Campaign
1. In Gmail, click Compose
2. Write subject: "DevOps Engineer | LogicMonitor Expert | Azure + Ansible"
3. Write body (use template from Cold_Email_Templates.md)
4. In the "To" field, add: `ankur.tewari@logicmonitor.com, swati.shukla@logicmonitor.com`
5. Click GMass → "Schedule" → set for tomorrow 8:00 AM IST
6. Enable "Follow-ups" → "If no reply, send follow-up in 3 days"

### Step 4: Track Results
- Open Gmail → Click "GMass" tab in sidebar
- You'll see: Sent: 2, Opens: 1, Replies: 0, Clicks: 0
- When someone opens → you get a notification
- When someone replies → GMass auto-stops follow-ups for that person

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem:** Hunter.io says no emails found for company
**Solution:** Try Apollo.io instead - it has a bigger database

**Problem:** GMass not showing in Gmail
**Solution:** Refresh Gmail tab. Check Chrome extensions. Reinstall GMass.

**Problem:** Email bounces back
**Solution:** Email was wrong. Try: firstname.lastname@company.com vs firstname@company.com vs flastname@company.com

**Problem:** LinkedIn sales navigator too expensive
**Solution:** Use free LinkedIn search: search "Talent Acquisition Pune DevOps" and filter by People
